Everything you need to run
your manufacturing operations

From raw material procurement to finished goods delivery — track every detail with precision and confidence.

Predictive Maintenance
Proactive maintenance scheduling identifies equipment wear patterns and alerts you before failures happen. Analyzes vibration, temperature, and cycle data to reduce unplanned downtime by up to 40%.
Production Optimization
Real-time production scheduling optimizes machine utilization, labor allocation, and material flow across all workcenters. Drag-and-drop planning with capacity-aware constraint solving.
Inventory Intelligence
Multi-warehouse tracking with demand forecasting, automated reorder points, and smart inventory optimization. Barcode and RFID scanning with lot and serial number tracking.
Quality Control
Advanced defect detection with real-time quality monitoring, statistical process control, and automated inspection workflows. Catch issues before they become costly recalls.
Machine Monitoring
Real-time equipment monitoring with IoT integration, vibration analysis, and automated alerts for abnormal operating conditions. OPC-UA and MQTT protocol support out of the box.
Smart Scheduling
Intelligent production scheduling that considers machine capacity, material availability, and delivery deadlines simultaneously. Automatically proposes alternatives when conflicts arise.
